About San Juan

About

San Juan in La Union harmoniously blends a relaxed vibe with energetic activity. Renowned as the surfing capital of the northern Philippines, it boasts a vibrant pottery industry. Conveniently situated along the Manila North Road, its accessibility has improved, with a journey from Manila taking around 4 hours via enhanced road networks. Hosting the popular Surfing Break in October, it attracts local and foreign tourists, offering two surfing seasons. With a population of 37,188 and an average annual income of P59,612,510.00, San Juan excels in water sports, especially in barangay Urbiztondo. Famed for its pottery craftsmanship, this 5,712-hectare town offers a serene community and active beach lifestyle, providing an escape from daily life. Notable landmarks like the Municipal Hall add to its charm.
