Content

Home page

San Juan, La Union, situated on the northern coast of the Philippines, is a hidden gem ready for exploration. Whether you're an adventurous traveler, an explorer of new horizons, or a passionate surfer in pursuit of the perfect wave and to discover history, you've landed at the perfect destination. Our website serves as your portal to uncover the awe-inspiring beauty, dynamic culture, and thrilling adventures that San Juan has to offer. Renowned globally for its world-class surfing spots and water-based activities, it's your guide to an adrenaline-fueled journey, offering the finest surf breaks, exciting water escapades, and an opportunity to embrace the mighty Pacific Ocean. Besides surfing, San Juan provides opportunities for snorkeling, paddleboarding, hiking, and more. Our extensive guides present you with exhilarating alternatives to ensure your trip is genuinely memorable.

Attractions

San Juan, La Union, is an enchanting destination that beckons travelers with its alluring array of attractions. Among its most captivating features are the pristine beaches, where golden sands meet the inviting turquoise waters, creating an idyllic paradise for beach lovers and water sports enthusiasts alike. Whether you're a sun-worshiper seeking the perfect tan or a thrill-seeker looking for your next aquatic adventure, the beaches of San Juan provide an irresistible draw. These sun-kissed shores offer more than just a place to soak up the sun. San Juan is hailed as the surfing capital of northern Philippines, attracting wave-riders from around the world. With its world-class surf breaks and consistent waves, it's a surfer's dream come true. Whether you're a seasoned surfer or a beginner eager to catch your first wave, San Juan's beaches provide the ideal setting for an unforgettable surfing experience. For those who seek adventure beyond the surf, San Juan offers a plethora of exciting activities. The town is a gateway to thrilling hiking trails that wind through lush, tropical landscapes. These trails lead to hidden waterfalls, adding an exciting dimension to your visit. Exploring the natural beauty of San Juan through its hiking trails allows you to immerse yourself in the stunning scenery, observe local wildlife, and discover the serenity of cascading waterfalls concealed within the heart of the jungle. With its perfect blend of natural beauty, thrilling activities, and a lively cultural scene, San Juan, La Union, has something to offer every traveler. Whether you're lounging on the beach, riding the waves, or embarking on a hiking expedition, your journey in San Juan promises an unforgettable experience, leaving you with cherished memories and a longing to return to this coastal paradise.

About

San Juan in La Union is a diverse town that perfectly balances a laidback atmosphere with vibrant activity. It's famous for its pottery industry and holds the title of the surfing capital of the northern Philippines. Located along the Manila North Road, San Juan is bordered by Bacnotan to the north, San Gabriel and Bagulin to the east, San Fernando City to the south, and the West Philippine Sea to the west. Traveling to San Juan has become more convenient, with the journey from Manila now taking around 4 hours, thanks to improved road networks like NLEX, SCTEX, and TPLEX. The town hosts the popular Surfing Break in October, attracting both local and foreign tourists. San Juan is a haven for surf enthusiasts, offering two surfing seasons from July to October and November to March. As a second-class municipality with an average annual income of P59,612,510.00 and a population of 37,188, San Juan is renowned for its surfing and various water sports activities, particularly in the vibrant barangay Urbiztondo. The town is also celebrated for its exceptional craftsmanship in pottery. It provides a serene community environment combined with an active beach lifestyle, making this 5,712-hectare town a truly beautiful escape from the daily hustle and bustle of life. Notable landmarks like the old-designed Municipal Hall building add to the town's charm and character.
